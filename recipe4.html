Grilled Chicken

⏱ Prep: 15 min 🍳 Cook: 30 min Medium 👤 Serves: 2

🧂 Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

📋 Instructions

  1. Mix olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, thyme, oregano, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Coat chicken breasts with the marinade. Let it marinate for at least 20 minutes.
  3. Preheat a grill pan or outdoor grill on medium-high heat.
  4. Grill chicken for 6–7 minutes per side until fully cooked (internal temp: 75°C).
  5. Let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing. Serve with salad or rice.
