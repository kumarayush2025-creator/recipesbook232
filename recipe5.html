Fudgy Chocolate Brownies

⏱ Prep: 15 min 🍳 Bake: 25 min Easy 👤 Makes: 9 pieces

🧂 Ingredients

  • 100g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 100g butter
  • 150g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 80g all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

📋 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C. Grease a square baking pan and line with parchment paper.
  2. Melt butter and chocolate together in a bowl over hot water (or microwave). Let it cool slightly.
  3. Whisk in sugar, then add eggs one at a time. Add vanilla extract.
  4. Fold in flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Do not overmix.
  5. Pour batter into the pan. Bake for 22–25 minutes.
  6. Cool completely before cutting into squares. Enjoy!
