Paneer Butter Masala

⏱ Prep: 15 min 🍳 Cook: 25 min Medium 👤 Serves: 3

🧂 Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp kashmiri red chili powder
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander to garnish

📋 Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan. Add onion and cook until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Pour in tomato puree, add chili powder and salt. Cook for 8 minutes until oil separates.
  4. Add garam masala and fresh cream. Stir well and simmer for 3 minutes.
  5. Add paneer cubes. Cook gently for 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with coriander. Serve with naan or rice.
