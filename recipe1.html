Classic Spaghetti Pasta

⏱ Prep: 10 min 🍳 Cook: 20 min Easy 👤 Serves: 2

🧂 Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese (to serve)
  • Fresh basil leaves

📋 Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente (8–10 min). Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan. Add garlic and onion, sauté for 3 minutes until soft.
  3. Add tomato puree and oregano. Cook on medium heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Mix the spaghetti into the sauce.
  5. Serve hot, topped with Parmesan cheese and fresh basil.
